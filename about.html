Dr. Samer Al-Salman

Orthopedic and joint surgery consultant

Dr. Samer Al-Salman

Dr. Samer Al-Salman is a consultant orthopedic surgeon who specializes in pediatric orthopedic surgery. The doctor has more than 14 years of experience in the field of orthopedics. He also excelled greatly in knee and shoulder surgery and has treated many intractable cases during his career.


Specialties

    Knee and joint stiffness.
    Osteoporosis, deformity and bone fractures.
    Sports injuries.
    Shoulder joint and recurrent dislocation.
    Laparoscopic treatment of tendon rupture.
    Hand and shoulder bones.
    Foot and ankle bones.

Work in

  • Orthopedic Resident - Jordan Hospital
  • Pediatric Orthopedic fellowship - Yonsei University Severance Hospital
  • Orthopedic Specialist - Suliman Alhabib Hospital
  • Orthopedic Consultant - Makkah Medical Hospital
